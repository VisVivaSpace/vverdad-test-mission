## Science Objectives


- **SO-1:** Determine the elemental and mineralogical composition of the Phobos surface
- **SO-2:** Return a sample of Phobos regolith for laboratory analysis on Earth
- **SO-3:** Characterize the surface morphology and geology of Phobos at sub-meter resolution
